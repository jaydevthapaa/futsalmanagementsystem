Our Story

JustFutsalThings was born from a simple idea: making futsal accessible to everyone. As passionate futsal players ourselves, we experienced the frustration of finding and booking quality grounds. We decided to create a solution that would connect futsal lovers with the best facilities in their area.

Today, we're proud to serve individual and organizations lovers of the game, helping them find their perfect playing space and create unforgettable memories on the court locally.
